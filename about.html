About the Township Trustee Office

The Township Trustee serves the community by administering township assistance, helping residents connect with available services, managing local responsibilities, and maintaining transparency in township government.

Mission

To serve residents with fairness, accountability, and compassion while providing essential township services.

Responsibilities

Administering assistance, supporting residents, maintaining cemeteries, and sharing township information.
