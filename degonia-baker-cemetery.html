Cemetery Details

Degonia / Baker Cemetery

Location & Directions

From Boonville, go East on Highway 62 towards Tennyson. The highway sharply curves to the left at approximately five miles from Boonville. Continue on Highway 62 and continue through the next sharp curve to the right. Continue about one-half mile and the cemetery is on the right before you get to the newly built Baker Chapel Church.
